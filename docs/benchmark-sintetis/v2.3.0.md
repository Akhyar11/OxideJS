## Major Improvements Over v2.2.8

- **RNN Family Speedup**: ~60% increase in training throughput (800 s/s -> 1300 s/s).
- **Fused Foward/Backward**: Eliminated multiple NAPI calls per sequence step by moving BPTT loops into Rust.
- **GRU Fix**: Corrected reset-gate math allowing GRU to converge properly in native mode.
- **Memory Efficiency**: Reduced GC pressure by utilizing pre-allocated native Float32Array buffers.

## Interpretation

The 2.3.0 release represents a massive architectural milestone for the recurrent family. By moving the entire BPTT sequence into native Rust, we've largely eliminated the "NAPI bottleneck". The convergence is perfect, and the speed is now approaching the theoretical limits of multi-threaded CPU training for these dimensions.
